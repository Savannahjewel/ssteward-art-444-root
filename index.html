What are your goals post grad?

When I get my bachelor's degree I would love to find a design agency that aligns with my values and fits into my aesthetic design wise. Ideally I would love to work in the branding and packaging space for breweries or the beauty industry.

I believe what separates me as a designer is my ability to use color and branding in an effective way that represents the brand. I love whimsical and unique designs and I don’t shy away from bold.

Point blank, why I think I am a great designer and asset to any team is my creativity and inspiration has no limits. I will be excited to create your brand for you and I will work well with any team to execute your vision.
